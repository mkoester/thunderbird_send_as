Feature 1: Auto-Reply with Alias

Automatically sets your From address to match aliases when replying to emails. Always active - no configuration needed!

How it works: When you reply to an email sent to user+shopping@domain.com, the extension automatically sets your From address to user+shopping@domain.com.
